Regular expressions
===================

Lookahead
=========

<http://www.regular-expressions.info/lookaround.html#lookahead>

Positive
---------

    foo(?=bar)

Negative
--------

    foo(?!bar)

Lookbehind
==========

<http://www.regular-expressions.info/lookaround.html#lookbehind>

Positive
---------

    (?<=bar)foo

Negative
--------

    (?<!bar)foo
